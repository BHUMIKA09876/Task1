About Us

Canvas & Soul is a creative space for artists, learners, and admirers. We offer expert-led art courses and also feature monthly exhibitions showcasing emerging talent.

Our mission is to inspire creativity and foster a community of art enthusiasts.

Join us in our journey to explore the world of art!

Our Services

🖼️ Monthly Art Exhibitions

Showcasing original work by emerging and professional artists every month.


🖌️ Custom Art Commissions

Portraits, abstract pieces, and spiritual art designed just for you by Canvas & Soul artists.

🌿 Art Therapy Sessions

Gentle, healing art sessions led by certified therapists to help you relax and reconnect.
